Redesigning the web for Dagens Nyheter, Sweden's leading morning newspaper

Role: Interaction Designer | Agency: Doberman

What We Did

Doberman helped Dagens Nyheter to once again design their online presence - web, mobile & iOS. We redesigned the web to be more engaging and to strengthen the news experience with it's constant updates. We did this by increasing the news pulse, emphasizing stories, highlighting photos and DN's most prominent writers. The mobile experience was redesigned from the ground up. The main objectives was to make all of dn.se's content available on the mobile site and with a designed that allowed this process to be 100% automated.

The Results

Since the release of the new DN mobile web site, it has more than doubled it's unique users and has been nominated for best mobile web by the Swedish Design Prize. DN’s web service was also acknowledged as Sweden’s best news site by the prominent magazine Internetworld. The financial results for DN.se after the re-design broke all previous records.

  • www.dn.se
  • mobil.dn.se
  • DN.se Mobil @ App Store

My Part

As part of the small team (four persons only) at Doberman, I was deeply involved in all three projects. I was the sole Interaction Designer and my responsibilities ranged all the way from concept development to QA.

Creating the e-book service Dito for KF Media, one of Sweden's larges book retailer

Role: Interaction Designer | Agency: Doberman

What We Did

Doberman helped KF Media innovate and design their offer. Working in close collaboration, we began by customer journey-mapping both physical and digital interactions to identify key opportunities for how to deliver an excellent service experience. Doberman designed the brand “Dito” and a complete integrated service ecosystem consisting of an online bookstore, and intuitive e-book apps for tablets and smart phones. Doberman also supported KF Media’s staff education program in rolling out the service to stores nationwide.

The Results

Dito’s launch, conceived and developed by Doberman, was met by strong media interest at the national book fair in Gothenburg. Dito has defined a new stage for e-books in Sweden and disrupted the market—the launch instantly established Akademibokhandeln and Bokus as Sweden’s largest e-book retailers. www.dito.se

My Part

As an Interaction designer I was deeply involved in both the web site and the apps.
